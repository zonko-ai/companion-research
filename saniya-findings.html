Three Things to Do

1. Fix the opening

82.7% never reply. But Feb 9 cohort had 31.4% activation — proving it works with the right audience. Test: voice note opener, a specific question about their city/work, suggested reply buttons. The hook must happen in messages 5–20 or the conversation dies.

2. Make responses richer

Saniya writes 322 chars avg in deep conversations, 46 chars in shallow. 7x difference. Low-Effort Response is flagged in 11.7% of power users. The fix: enforce minimum response richness, always end with a specific question about THEIR life, never give a generic reply to an engaged user.

3. Double down on Emotional Companion

44% multi-day return. 35% Positive Outcome. The users who feel heard come back. The users who come for entertainment have a session and leave. Build for the farmer, the toll collector, the barber — the man nobody else asks about.
