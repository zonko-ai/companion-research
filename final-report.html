13. Three Things

1. Fix the opening. 80.9% never send a message. Test voice note openers, specific cultural questions, suggested reply buttons. The Feb 9 cohort had 31% activation — it can work.
2. Double down on Emotional Companion. 44% multi-day return. 35% Positive Outcome. 68% activation. The data picks the direction: build for the man who wants to feel heard, not the one who wants entertainment.
3. Make voice the default. 24,160 voice notes vs 400 images. 69% of 100+ msg users send media. The deepest user sent 6,413 voice notes in 20 days. Voice is how real engagement happens.
