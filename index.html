The Three Hard Problems (Our Moat)

01

Cultural-Linguistic Intelligence

Mainstream LLMs have 0.01% Indic content. 40% misrecognition for regional accents. We're building for the way 350M+ people actually talk — not translated English, not formal Hindi, but street Hinglish.

02

Structured Personal Memory

After 8-12 turns, persona consistency degrades 30%+. We're building memory that understands joint families, festival cycles, and health histories across generations.

03

Proactive Emotional Intelligence

Every AI companion waits. Real companions reach out. We're solving the hardest calibration problem: knowing when someone needs to hear from you.

The Milestones

The Stage Gates

Each stage has one question, one test, one kill condition.

10 DAU "Does anyone care?"

Test: 15+ messages in 5 minutes. 50% unprompted return in 24 hours.

Kill: <40% reaching 10 messages.

1,000 DAU "Is this a habit?"

Test: D30 retention >25%. Vohra PMF score >40%.

10,000 DAU "Does trust unlock utility?"

Test: Utility feature adoption >40% among retained users.

100,000 DAU "Platform play?"

Test: Contextual commerce converts at >5x industry average.
